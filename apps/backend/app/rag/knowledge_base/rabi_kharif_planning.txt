Title: Seasonal Crop Planning for Kharif and Rabi

Kharif planning should consider monsoon onset, rainfall distribution and waterlogging risk.
Rabi planning should focus on residual soil moisture, irrigation availability and cold stress windows.
Sequence crops to improve soil fertility, for example cereal-pulse rotations.
Select varieties based on district-level recommendations and expected sowing window.
Late sowing generally reduces yield potential; prioritize timely land preparation and seed availability.

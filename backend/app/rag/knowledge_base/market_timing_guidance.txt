Title: Mandi Market Timing Guidance

Farmgate and mandi prices are seasonal and affected by arrivals, storage and transport constraints.
Use short-horizon forecasts (7 to 30 days) for tactical selling decisions and long-horizon forecasts for planning.
Avoid panic selling immediately after harvest when arrivals peak and prices are depressed.
If storage quality can be maintained, staggered selling can reduce downside risk.
Track market-specific trends because price movement differs across mandis for the same commodity.
